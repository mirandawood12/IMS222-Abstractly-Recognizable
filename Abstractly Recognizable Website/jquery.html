Web design is the art and science of creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. It involves a combination of creativity, technical skills, and an understanding of user experience (UX) principles. Designers work with a variety of tools and technologies to structure content, choose color palettes, select fonts, and implement interactive elements.

The ultimate goal of web design is to create websites that are not only visually engaging but also easy to navigate and provide a seamless user experience. By combining technical expertise with creative flair, web designers bring ideas to life on the internet.
